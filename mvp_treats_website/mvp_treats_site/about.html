About MVP Treats

A student-led brand with a simple mission

MVP Treats delivers premium, single-ingredient freeze-dried chicken livers, hearts, and feet, along with wholesome baked dog biscuits crafted for owners who refuse to compromise on quality. Our products contain no additives, no artificial flavors, and no hidden fillers; just pure, protein-rich organ meats that pets naturally crave. Our freeze-drying process preserves nutrients and flavor without relying on chemicals or shortcuts, giving pets a cleaner, more nutritious treat than many mass-produced alternatives.

A

Brand story

MVP Treats started when the owner, Aidan Huegen, decided that pets were not getting the nutrition they deserve with store-bought treats. This led to the idea of making transparent, clean ingredient treats for of your All-Stars.

B

What makes it different

MVP Treats sources ingredients locally, without any chemicals, preservatives, or additives that would not benefit your companion.

C

Community connection

We thrive to make your furry friend happy, support local businesses, and making you feel good about your choice with our treats.
